1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

The Contractor agrees to provide independent dispatch and broker agent services for FleetFlow Transportation, including but not limited to:

  • Load Dispatching: Coordinate and dispatch loads to qualified carriers
  • Broker Operations: Negotiate rates, manage shipper relationships, and facilitate freight transactions
  • Customer Service: Provide professional communication with shippers, carriers, and internal teams
  • Documentation: Maintain accurate records of all transactions and communications
  • Compliance: Ensure all operations comply with DOT regulations and company policies
  • Technology Utilization: Effectively use FleetFlow's proprietary systems and software
2. INDEPENDENT CONTRACTOR STATUS

The Contractor is an independent contractor and not an employee of FleetFlow Transportation. The Contractor:

  • Has the right to control and determine the method, manner, and means of performing the contracted services
  • Is responsible for providing their own equipment, workspace, and utilities
  • May work for other companies provided no conflict of interest exists
  • Is responsible for all tax obligations and business expenses
  • Is not entitled to employee benefits, workers' compensation, or unemployment compensation
3. COMPENSATION STRUCTURE
Service Type Commission Rate Minimum Performance
Dispatch Services 15% of gross margin per load 20 loads per month
Broker Agent Services 20% of gross margin per load 15 loads per month
Customer Acquisition 25% of gross margin (first 6 months) 2 new customers per quarter
Performance Bonus Additional 5% for exceeding targets 150% of monthly minimum

Payment Terms: Compensation paid bi-weekly, 15 days after load completion and payment collection.

4. PERFORMANCE STANDARDS

The Contractor must maintain the following performance standards:

  • Response Time: Respond to all communications within 2 hours during business hours
  • Accuracy: Maintain 95% accuracy in load documentation and data entry
  • Customer Satisfaction: Achieve minimum 4.5/5 rating in monthly customer surveys
  • Load Completion: Achieve 98% on-time delivery rate for dispatched loads
  • Compliance: Zero compliance violations or regulatory issues
5. CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-DISCLOSURE
CRITICAL: This section is legally binding and essential for protecting business operations.

The Contractor agrees to:

  • Maintain strict confidentiality of all customer information, pricing, and business operations
  • Not disclose any proprietary information, trade secrets, or business strategies
  • Return all company materials and delete all confidential information upon termination
  • Not use confidential information for personal gain or competitive advantage
  • Report any security breaches or unauthorized access immediately
6. NON-COMPETE AND NON-SOLICITATION

During the term of this agreement and for 12 months thereafter, the Contractor agrees to:

  • Not directly compete with FleetFlow Transportation in the same geographic markets
  • Not solicit or attempt to divert any FleetFlow customers or carriers
  • Not recruit or hire any FleetFlow employees or contractors
  • Not establish a competing freight brokerage or dispatch operation
7. TECHNOLOGY AND SYSTEMS

The Contractor must:

  • Use only FleetFlow-approved software and systems for all business operations
  • Maintain secure internet connection and modern computer equipment
  • Complete all required technology training and certifications
  • Report technical issues immediately to IT support
  • Follow all cybersecurity protocols and data protection guidelines
8. INSURANCE AND LIABILITY

The Contractor must maintain:

  • Professional liability insurance with minimum $1,000,000 coverage
  • General liability insurance with minimum $500,000 coverage
  • Cyber liability insurance with minimum $250,000 coverage
  • Errors and omissions insurance for all dispatch and broker activities

FleetFlow Transportation is not liable for any damages, losses, or claims arising from the Contractor's services.

9. TERMINATION

This agreement may be terminated:

  • By either party with 30 days written notice
  • Immediately for breach of contract, non-performance, or violation of policies
  • Immediately for any criminal activity or regulatory violation
  • Upon failure to maintain required insurance coverage

Upon termination, all obligations regarding confidentiality and non-compete remain in effect.

10. COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

The Contractor must:

  • Maintain current knowledge of all DOT and FMCSA regulations
  • Complete annual compliance training and certifications
  • Report any regulatory changes or compliance issues immediately
  • Maintain all required licenses and certifications
  • Submit to periodic compliance audits and performance reviews
11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

Any disputes arising from this agreement will be resolved through:

  • Good faith negotiation between the parties
  • Binding arbitration if negotiation fails
  • Applicable state and federal law
12. GENERAL PROVISIONS
  • This agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties
  • Any modifications must be in writing and signed by both parties
  • If any provision is deemed unenforceable, the remainder remains in effect
  • This agreement is governed by the laws of the state where FleetFlow is incorporated
