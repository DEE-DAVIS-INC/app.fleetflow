1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into to protect confidential and proprietary information that may be disclosed by FleetFlow Transportation to the Receiving Party in connection with:

  • Independent contractor services for dispatch and broker operations
  • Training and onboarding processes
  • Access to proprietary systems and technology
  • Customer and carrier relationship management
  • Business strategy and operational procedures
2. DEFINITION OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:
  • Customer Information: Names, contacts, shipping patterns, rates, and preferences
  • Carrier Information: Carrier networks, performance data, and contractual terms
  • Financial Information: Pricing strategies, profit margins, and revenue data
  • Technology: Software systems, algorithms, and proprietary platforms
  • Business Strategy: Market analysis, growth plans, and competitive intelligence
  • Operational Data: Load patterns, route optimization, and efficiency metrics
  • Employee Information: Personnel records, compensation, and organizational structure
  • Trade Secrets: Any proprietary methods, processes, or know-how
3. CONFIDENTIALITY OBLIGATIONS

The Receiving Party agrees to:

  • Maintain Confidentiality: Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential
  • Limited Use: Use Confidential Information solely for authorized business purposes
  • No Disclosure: Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written consent
  • Protection: Take reasonable precautions to protect Confidential Information from unauthorized access
  • No Reproduction: Not copy, reproduce, or distribute Confidential Information except as necessary for authorized purposes
4. EXCEPTIONS TO CONFIDENTIALITY

The obligations of confidentiality do not apply to information that:

  • Is or becomes publicly available through no breach of this Agreement
  • Was known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure
  • Is independently developed without use of Confidential Information
  • Is required to be disclosed by law or court order (with prior notice to FleetFlow)
  • Is authorized for disclosure in writing by FleetFlow
5. SECURITY MEASURES
MANDATORY SECURITY REQUIREMENTS:

The Receiving Party must implement and maintain:

  • Physical Security: Secure storage of all confidential documents and materials
  • Digital Security: Password protection, encryption, and secure networks
  • Access Controls: Limit access to Confidential Information on a need-to-know basis
  • Employee Training: Ensure all personnel understand confidentiality requirements
  • Incident Reporting: Immediately report any security breaches or unauthorized access
6. RETURN OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

Upon termination of the business relationship or upon FleetFlow's request, the Receiving Party must:

  • Return all documents, materials, and records containing Confidential Information
  • Delete all electronic copies of Confidential Information
  • Provide written certification of compliance with return requirements
  • Ensure all personnel under their control comply with return requirements
7. CONSEQUENCES OF BREACH

The Receiving Party acknowledges that:

  • Breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to FleetFlow
  • Monetary damages may be inadequate to compensate for such harm
  • FleetFlow is entitled to seek injunctive relief and specific performance
  • The Receiving Party may be liable for all damages, costs, and attorney fees
  • Violation may result in immediate termination of business relationship
8. DURATION OF OBLIGATIONS

The obligations under this Agreement shall:

  • Commence upon signing this Agreement
  • Continue for the duration of the business relationship
  • Survive termination of the business relationship for a period of 5 years
  • Continue indefinitely for trade secrets and proprietary information
9. PERMITTED DISCLOSURES

The Receiving Party may disclose Confidential Information only to:

  • Employees who have signed confidentiality agreements
  • Professional advisors bound by confidentiality (attorneys, accountants)
  • Third parties with prior written consent from FleetFlow
  • Authorized representatives in connection with permitted business activities
10. COMPLIANCE MONITORING

FleetFlow reserves the right to:

  • Monitor compliance with this Agreement
  • Conduct periodic security audits
  • Request reports on information handling practices
  • Investigate suspected breaches of confidentiality
11. GENERAL PROVISIONS
  • Governing Law: This Agreement is governed by applicable state and federal law
  • Jurisdiction: Any disputes will be resolved in courts where FleetFlow is incorporated
  • Entire Agreement: This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding regarding confidentiality
  • Modifications: Any changes must be in writing and signed by both parties
  • Severability: If any provision is unenforceable, the remainder remains in effect
